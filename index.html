About Us

We are the student branch for IEEE, the world's largest organization for technical professionals, at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. We help undergraduate and graduate students connect with industry professionals, faculty members, and students.

Learn more

Get Involved

We provide corporate recruiting events, cutting-edge research talks, and casual social events throughout the semester for our members. Our organization is committed to providing you everything you need to launch a career, get involved in research, or find fellow students with similar interests.

Become a Member

No matter what your background, if you are a student at the University of Michigan, you are eligible for active IEEE student membership. Learn about your first steps to earn access to global resources and connections to help you succeed in wherever you go.
